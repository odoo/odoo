Presentations, Videos, Documents, Infographics

Publish your presentations, videos, documents, infographics, etc.

Odoo presentations lets you upload files privately or publicly in the following file formats: PowerPoint, PDF, Keynote, Video or OpenDocument presentations.

This module is originally meant to be used by businesses to share slides among employees more easily. It also provides the users with the ability to rate, comment, share the uploaded content and see detailed statistics like total views, web-views, embedded-views, likes and dislikes, total comments, total social-shares and shares on individual social site.
