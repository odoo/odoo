Odoo VOIP

Automate Calls, Transfer, Logs and Mails

Manage your phonecalls directly from Odoo. Call your customers, manage a call queue, log your calls, schedule calls.

Call In One Click

The Odoo VOIP allows you to make calls from your browser to your customers. Once your call queue is filled, select a phonecall and call it in one click.

Automate Calls

Increase your number of calls with the automate calls. You don't need to dial the number anymore, process the all queue in one click. Once one call is finished and looged, the next call is triggered.

Log Calls

Keep a historic of all your calls. After each call, the log window is opened. You can reschedule a call, define a new action or describe the call.

Send Email And Transfer Calls

The panel let you transfer the call or send an email to the customer. The emails can use templates defined perviously by the salesman. You can also reach customer or opportunity information.
