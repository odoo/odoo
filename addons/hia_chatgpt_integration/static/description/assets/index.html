ChatGPT Integration

The integration of ChatGPT within Odoo enhances internal communication and productivity by providing employees with a powerful AI assistant. This setup allows users to interact with ChatGPT individually or within a group channel, offering general assistance and fostering collaboration without accessing sensitive HR, sales, or other Odoo-related data.

Why HIA ChatGPT Integration ?

ChatGPT integration in Odoo streamlines communication and boosts productivity,
offering round-the-clock assistance and fostering collaboration without compromising data security.
