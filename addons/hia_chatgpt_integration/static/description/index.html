ChatGPT Integration

The integration of ChatGPT within Odoo enhances internal communication and productivity by providing employees with a powerful AI assistant. This setup allows users to interact with ChatGPT individually or within a group channel, offering general assistance and fostering collaboration without accessing sensitive HR, sales, or other sensitive data.

Why ChatGPT Integration ?

Integrating ChatGPT into Odoo streamlines communication and boosts
productivity without any external package dependencies, offering round-the-clock
assistance and fostering collaboration while prioritizing data security.
