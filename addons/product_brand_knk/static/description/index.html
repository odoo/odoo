Product Brand Manager

Manage multiple brands and their products effortlessly. Assign brands to products and allow customers to filter by brand on the shop page.

Create and Assign Brand


Navigate to Sales > Configuration >Products > Product Brand.

Create New Brands here.


You can also create new products for a particular brand from here.

Now, assign a brand to the product in the products section.

Now, on the shop page of the website, you can find the brand filter.


You can also filter products by selecting multiple brands.

On the Brand page of the website, you can find all the brands.


It also displays how many products are assigned to each brand.

By clicking on a brand, you will be redirected to the shop page where the filter is applied automatically.

Key Features

Easily manage and assign brands to products.

Filter products by brand on the shop page.
