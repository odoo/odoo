Point of Sale Product & Product Variant Multiple Barcode Odoo Apps

Allow to Create Multiple Barcode for Product & Product Variant with POS Odoo Apps

Product and Product Variant Multi Barcode for POS Odoo App is allowed to create a multiple barcode for a single product and product variant under 'Barcodes' tab. Users can also easily search for that product and product variant based on multiple barcodes for sales order, purchase order, invoice, vendor bill, delivery and shipment and Point of sales. You can also search for a product on product view with assigned barcode , you can search product with any barcode number from multiple barcodes of that product or variant, also users can search or scan product and product variant on point of sales with assigned multiple barcode.

Features

Add Multiple Barcode for Single Product

User can add multiple barcode for both single product and product variant.

Add Multiple Barcode for Single Product Variant

User can add multiple barcode for single product under barcode tab.

Search Product or Product Variant by Multi Barcode on POS, Sales, Purchase

User can search or scan product or product template by multi barcode on point of sale, sale order, purchase order.

Search Product or Variant by Multi Barcode on Invoice, Bill, Deliver, Receipt.

User can search product or variant by multi barcode on customer invoice, vendor bill, delivery and receipt.

Unique Multi Barcode Per Product & Product Variant

Each product and product variant has unique multiple barcode, raise warning if same adding for other variant.

Scan Product or Product Variant by Multi Barcode on POS

User can scan product or variant by multi barcode on point of sale.
