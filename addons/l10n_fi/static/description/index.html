Finnish Localization

Chart of Accounts, taxes, business ID and other features

Standard Business Reporting (=SBR or Raportointikoodisto) is a standard code set making Finnish official reporting easier by supplementing the common chart of accounts.

Taxes

The following domestic taxes are included:

  • Purchase VAT 24 %, Goods
  • Purchase VAT 24 %, Services
  • Purchase VAT 14 %, Goods
  • Purchase VAT 14 %, Services
  • Purchase VAT 10 %, Goods
  • Purchase VAT 10 %, Services
  • Sales VAT 24 %, Goods
  • Sales VAT 24 %, Services
  • Sales VAT 14 %, Goods
  • Sales VAT 14 %, Services
  • Sales VAT 10 %, Goods
  • Sales VAT 10 %, Services

The following intra-community taxes are included:

  • Purchase VAT 24 %, Goods (carry out and deduct)
  • Purchase VAT 24 %, Services (carry out and deduct)
  • Purchase VAT 14 %, Goods (carry out and deduct)
  • Purchase VAT 14 %, Services (carry out and deduct)
  • Purchase VAT 10 %, Goods (carry out and deduct)
  • Purchase VAT 10 %, Services (carry out and deduct)
  • Sales VAT 0 %, Goods
  • Sales VAT 0 %, Services
Other taxes:
  • Sales VAT 0 % (export outside EU and other tax-free sales that must be reported in the tax report)

New Fields

This module adds the Finnish business ID (Y-tunnus) for customers and suppliers and makes sure it's valid. It also lets you choose which invoice reference to use; RF creditor reference, Standard Finnish reference or a manual reference. The reference type can be set per company.

Note that this module does not alter the default invoice or email templates. To make the payment reference visible you must modify the templates.

Other New Fields:

  • EDI code for partner
