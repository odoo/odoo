SaaS Kit Custom Plans

Let your customers choose their desired apps from the Odoo Saas Plan!

Odoo SaaS Custom Plans facilitates you to offer flexibility to your clients so that they can pick the apps of their choice from the Odoo SaaS plan. The bill of apps purchased is added to the next upcoming billing cycle.

Customer satisfaction is key to a successful business; hence, when you run a business, you need to look for every possible way to meet your customers demand. It is basic human nature that people want to pay only for the things which they use.

Odoo Saas kit allows you to provide a Separate Odoo Environment to Each of your clients. However, by default your Odoo Saas customers do not have flexibility to choose the apps as per their requirements.

Want to provide Custom Apps selection features to your customer?

If yes then you can go for Odoo Saas Custom Plans extension to deliver Custom made saas instances to your clients. Odoo SaaS Custom Plans allows you to provide a feature to your clients so that they can pick the apps of their preference from the Odoo SaaS plan. The module fulfills the exact demand of your customers; hence, it is important for your business to chase the unstoppable growth.

Perks for Businesses to install Odoo Saas Custom Plans
  • Publish available Apps in Odoo Saas Kit.
  • You can deliver multiple versions of Odoo using one saas.
  • No need to Define Plan prior to selling Instances.
  • Allow you to define the layout of Apps Page.
  • Allow you to configure user based pricing/monthly based pricing.
Benefits of the Odoo Saas Custom Plans Model for your Customers
  • Free to choose his desired apps for his instance.
  • Can Add more apps in the running instance.
  • Select No. of Users of his own choice.
  • Free to choose his desired version of Odoo for his instance.
  • Create complete Instance just after completion of Payment and Selection of Domain Name.

Detailed Features List

Provide Custom Plan to Clients

  • Use Odoo Saas Custom Plan module to provide Custom App selection features to your clients.
  • Clients can Select and Pay only for those Apps which they want.
  • No Extra App will be delivered to the client.

Provide Odoo Version Selection Feature to clients

  • Configure Multiple Odoo Versions in Odoo saas.
  • Launch the configured versions for Clients.
  • Now clients have the feature to Choose the desired version odoo for his Instance.
  • Client instance will run on his selected odoo version irrespective of your main odoo version.

Clients have flexibility to Select Users

  • Admin can provide the feature to clients to purchase users with the apps.
  • Module provides the feature to purchase the required number of users for his instance.
  • Admin can also provide free users to Clients.

Different Layout for Apps Page

  • Admin can configure different layouts for apps page view.
  • Apps can be arranged in normal layout or Categorically.

Add Apps to Running Instance

  • Customer can Add More apps of his desire to the Running instance.
  • Selected Apps will get auto installed via crone.
  • Payment for added apps will be done in the next billing cycle.

Easy To Manage Billing Cycle

  • The cost of custom apps used is managed in the next billing cycle of the customer.
