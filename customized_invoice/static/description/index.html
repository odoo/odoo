Professional Invoice Report Templates - Odoo 9.0

Use this module to generate professional invoices that are attractive and bear your company colors and style. Choose from unlimited number of colors for Invoice text, company name, customer name and the background theme color matching your official company colors. Also choose the best template for use either as the default template for all invoices or for a particular invoice you generate. You have all the flexibility and choice in your hands!

Feature: Unlimited colors to pick for your invoice template

Unlike before, you have a choice of how you want your invoice to look before sending it to your customer. This module gives you the ability to pick the colors matching your official company colors. There are two options when choosing colors and company logo for your invoice.

  • Choose the DEFAULT colors, company logo and invoice template for ALL your invoices
  • Choose the colors, company logo and template for every Invoice you generate
Basically you have the freedom to configure how your invoice will look like before printing; unlike the default odoo invoice which you cannot configure any color or change the logo or template.This module is addressing this issue.

Settings: How to change colors, Logos and Invoice Templates

Templates: There are five different types of templates(more coming soon) to choose from

  • 1. Classic Invoice Template
  • 2. Modern Invoice Template
  • 3. Retro Invoice Template
  • 4. TVA Invoice Template
  • 4. Odoo Default Template

Colors and Logos: There are two ways to change invoice colors and logos

  • 1. You can change the Default Invoice Settings from the "Companies Form"; this will affect all invoices generated henceforth
  • 2. You can override the default settings by choosing colors and logo on the Invoice Form as you generate the invoice; This will affect the current invoice ONLY
Note that if you dont upload your logo, the default company logo set in odoo will be used.
